Who are the Sauti-Sol?

The sauti Sol are a Kenyan afro-pop band formed in Nairobi, Kenya by Bien-Aime Baraza, Polycarp Otieno, Willis Austin Chimano, Savara Mudigi. in 2005. Initially, the a cappella group guitarist Polycarp Otieno before they named themselves Sauti-Sol. Sauti-Sol released their debut studio album in 1 November 2008 Mwanzo. The Band has had several successful tours in Africa and Europe, tapped Kenya charts and gained international attention with shows in Europe and USA as well as television appearances andvarious accolades and nominations.
